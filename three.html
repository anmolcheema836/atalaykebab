The Origin Story

Not Fast Food.
Good Food, Fast.

Founded by Resul Atalay in 1988, we brought the authentic taste of Turkey to Thame and gave it a uniquely British twist. We don't believe in processed fillers. We stack our own meat, chop our own salads, and brew our secret sauce like a magic potion.

It's a family business, run with passion, consistency, and a love for the late-night crowd.
