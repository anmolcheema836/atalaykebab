Our Story

Good Food, Fast.

Founded by Resul Atalay in 1988, we set out to change the perception of the kebab. It’s not just late-night fuel; it’s a craft. We don't believe in processed fillers. We hand-stack our doner meat every single morning, chop our salads fresh, and serve every customer with the warmth of a family business.
