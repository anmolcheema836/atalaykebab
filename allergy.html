ALLERGEN GUIDE

IMPORTANT SAFETY ADVICE

Please always inform our staff of any allergies before ordering, even if you have eaten the dish before.

Our kitchen is small, and we handle Gluten, Milk, Eggs, Soya, Mustard, Sesame, and Sulphites. While we take care to prevent cross-contamination, we cannot guarantee that any item is 100% allergen-free.

Fryer Warning: Chips are cooked in the same oil as items containing Gluten (e.g., Popcorn Chicken).
