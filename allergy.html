ALLERGEN MATRIX

Detailed allergen information for every item on our menu.

IMPORTANT ADVICE

Please inform our staff of any allergies before ordering.
While we take every precaution, our kitchen handles Gluten, Milk, Eggs, Soya, Mustard, Sesame, and Sulphites. We cannot guarantee any item is 100% allergen-free due to the risk of cross-contamination.

Sauces: Please note that sauces are added separately and contain their own allergens (e.g., Yogurt Sauce contains Milk, Mayo contains Egg/Mustard).
